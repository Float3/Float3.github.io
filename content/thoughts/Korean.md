### Problem Consonants

endings in ㅅ ㅆ ㅊ ㅎ are pronounced "t"
endings in ㅇ are pronounced "ng"

## Accents

In some regional Korean accents, the polite ending "yo" (요) can be pronounced as "ye" (예). For example:
안녕하세요 → 안녕하세예
