## Japanese

### おう sound

a sequence of おう becomes おー
so
(き)のう as in 昨日 is pronounced きのー
行こう 行こー
会おう あおー
unless the お is the core vowel like in 匂う(におう) it won’t be におー

the same seems to be true for えい → えー
